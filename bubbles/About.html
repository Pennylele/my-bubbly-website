About Me

Hey there! If you you are reading this page, you probably mean to learn something about me. I want to tell you four things about myself — my name, my passion, my dream job, and my personality(No worries, it wouldn’t be that long).

My Chinese name is Le Fang, but I would love people to call me Penny, as I have developed great attachment to this name. Thanks to the Big Bang Theory! (Hope you have watched it, it’s fun.)

Different from that Penny in the Big Bang Theory, my passion lies in localization, technology, and languages. As a linguist, I have earned my MA TESOL degree in Michigan State University and have two-year Chinese teaching experience to both college students and 5-year-old kids. I am also earning my translation and localization management degree at Middlebury Institute of International Studies, which prepares me to be a localization professional. I am so excited that I am graduating this May and will be able to put what I have learned in school into practice.

My dream job is localization engineering and localization project management(especially in the area of software and website localization). As I have been exposing myself to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I am super interested in front-end web development. I am starting to try to build my own simple newbie website project.

For my personal traits, I am always excited to learn new skills and knowledge. I am not afraid of being exhausted, as long as I can learn new stuff. My goal is to enrich myself with all different knowledge and become a tech geek!

After reading this you may have known something about me…and yes you are right! I am wearing a big pair of glasses!
